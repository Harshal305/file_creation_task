India has been a federal republic since 1950, governed through a democratic parliamentary system.
 It is a pluralistic, multilingual and multi-ethnic society.
 India's population grew from 361 million in 1951 to over 1.4 billion in 2023.[63] During this time,
 its nominal per capita income increased from US$64 annually to US$2,601, and its literacy rate from 16.6% to 74%.
